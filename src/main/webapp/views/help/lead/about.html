About

  • Leads are people with whom you want to do business with. Navimate provides a way to create a lead directory and access it anytime to perform work. As a manager, you can send these leads to your representatives and track the work done in reports.
  • You can attach a title, description and contact information such as phone and email with a lead. You can also attach a location to the lead.
  • You can use your saved leads to create tasks and assign them to representatives. The lead information becomes visible in representative's app. Lead is shown on a map so that he can reach his destination quickly.
