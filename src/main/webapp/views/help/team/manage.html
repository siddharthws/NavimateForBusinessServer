Manage Team Members

  • Through the Navimate Dashboard, you can add / remove members in the team.
  • To add a team member
    • Go to Team Management
    • Click on Add Rep Button
    • Fill details (Phone Number, Name, Email (optional))
  • To remove a team member
    • Go to Team Management
    • Select the reps you want to remove using the checkboxes on left
    • Select Remove from Actions Dropdown
