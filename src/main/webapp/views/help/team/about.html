About

  • Navimate allows you to create a team using phone numbers and names. Using the dashboard, you can assign tasks to team, track each member's progress and get visit data regularly.
  • Your team members will use the Navimate For Business Android App .Team member's get notifications when tasks are assigned to them. They can view lead locations, route to desired meeting and submit forms after meetings.
  • You can track each member's work using Team Reports .
